Responsive Design Rule:

   2 Use flexible and relative units: Use flexible and relative units, such as percentages, ems, or rems, instead of fixed units like pixels, to ensure that your page elements scale proportionally with the viewport size.

    5 Test on different devices: Test your page on different devices and screen sizes to ensure that it looks good and functions properly on all devices.
